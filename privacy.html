Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to V-Invoice ("we", "our", "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information and your company's financial data. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection, storage, and usage when you use our mobile application ("App").

At V-Invoice, our philosophy is simple: Your data is yours. Our application is designed around local-first data principles.

2. Data Collection & Storage

Offline First: V-Invoice functions entirely offline. All data you input—including company settings, client details, products, invoices, payment histories, and digital signatures—is stored locally on your device.

We do not operate remote databases or cloud servers to store your business invoices. We do not sync, transmit, or harvest your financial documentation unless explicitly initiated by you (e.g., via the share or backup functions).

3. Use of Device Permissions

To provide full functionality, the App may request the following permissions:

  • Storage / Files accessing: Required to generate PDF files, process digital signatures, upload company logos, and handle JSON backup imports/exports.
  • Camera / Photos: Required only if you choose to directly snap a photo for a company logo or signature upload.

4. Third-Party Sharing

Because your data is stored securely on your local device, we do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties. Generating a PDF or sending a reminder uses your device's native sharing capabilities (e.g., Apple Share Sheet or Android Intent), which invokes third-party email or messaging clients of your choosing.

5. Data Backups

You have the option to export all data to a single JSON file for backup purposes. This file is generated locally on your device. You are responsible for safely storing this file on your preferred medium (icloud, google drive, local disk, etc.).

6. Terms of Service

By downloading and using V-Invoice, you agree that you are solely responsible for compliance with your local tax laws, invoicing regulations, and data persistence methodologies. The templates provided are guides and must be verified by you for official compliance where applicable.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the app support channels or by reaching out to your system administrator.
